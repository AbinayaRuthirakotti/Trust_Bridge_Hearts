Our Story & Mission

{% if trust.focus == 'Education & Shelter' or trust.focus == 'Education First' %}

Our core mission is to break the cycle of poverty through **uninterrupted education**. {% elif trust.focus == 'Healthcare & Nutrition' %}

{% elif trust.focus == 'Skill Development' %}
