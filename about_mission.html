Our Mandate: Erasing Doubt, Guaranteeing Impact

The Solution: The Direct Bridge

TBH serves as a clear, unblockable channel. Your donation is transferred directly to the verified Trust, and our platform forces the Trust to upload the verifiable proof—a receipt and photo—straight back to you.

This creates a secure, two-way loop of faith, bypassing all traditional barriers to accountability.

The Trust Bridge Hearts (TBH) Model

We provide the verifiable digital infrastructure. Our approach is inspired by the **Udhavum Ullangal** model: **Direct Support and Zero Ambiguity.**

100% Transparency Guaranteed

TBH ensures that 100% of your donation is forwarded. Accountability is ensured by mandatory **Expense Reporting**—Trusts submit the **actual receipt and photo proof** directly to your registered email.

The Challenge of Modern Philanthropy

For too long, the barrier between compassion and charitable giving has been **trust**. Donors often face uncertainty about where their money goes, leading to donor fatigue and skepticism toward genuine causes.

We recognize that genuine, local children's homes often lack the resources for sophisticated global reporting. Our mission is to bridge this gap by providing a verifiable, secure system that empowers small, high-impact trusts.

The Problem: A Broken Chain

The current model is a fragmented process: Donors give to intermediaries, who fund local trusts, creating a long, opaque chain where the impact proof is often lost.

This lack of a direct link between the giver and the outcome is the primary source of doubt and hesitation.
